Our Program

Hello and welcome to the After School Coding Club! This club is intended to provide and fun and interactive place where kids can learn about the wonderful world of computer programming. Our program is intended for kids aged 8-12 who have little to no experience with coding. We walk children through the basics of Scratch (a great introductory programming tool using interactive building blocks to model code) and help them build their confidence from week to week by incrementally building one the skills they acquired in the previous lesson. Kids love the graphic nature of scratch and love to explore all the amazing interactive components that can be built with just a few lessons.

About Us

Our school was founded by Frances Karulas, a seasoned Software Developer with over 15 years in the technology industry who has worked for large technology firms, Canada’s biggest banks, as well as one of Toronto’s hottest start-ups. Frances has a passion for programming and enjoys passing on this incredibly useful skill to the next generation of budding coders. She is committed to seeing new programmers build their repertoire of skills and gain confidence in the process. Some classes are run by Frances herself, while others are mentored by one of our other skilled developers on staff.
